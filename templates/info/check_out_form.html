At the end of your time working in the LCP, please remember to do all of the following before leaving:

  • Return keys that were issued to you, including door keys and keys to desks, drawers, and cabinets.
  • Return any books or other items that you've borrowed from Dr. Mark (or from others).
  • Leave Dr. Mark a copy of any publications (e.g., your thesis) related to your work in the LCP.
  • Archive research-related computer files and let Dr. Mark know where they're stored and how to retrieve them.
  • Delete personal and irrelevant files from computers you were using.
  • Clean out your desk and the area around it.
  • Check with Ken to confirm that you've done everything listed above. Then send Ken your end date, information about your electronic files, and any new contact information, using the form below:
