Welcome to the Laboratory for Computational Physiology. Listed below are steps to take upon joining the lab.

  1. If you have not done so already, check with the IMES administrative office in E25-510 to arrange for an MIT ID and a kerberos account, and to take care of paperwork.
  2. Read the information in the Introduction to the Laboratory for Computational Physiology.
  3. Follow the instructions in Section 2.3 of that document to complete EHS registration and COUHES training.
  4. Send an e-mail message to Felipe, Benjamin, and Ken with a couple of paragraphs describing who you are and what brings you to the LCP, who will be supervising your work here, and what you'll be doing.
  5. Fill out and submit the LCP registration form. Submitting the form will generate an e-mail message that will be sent to Felipe, Benjamin, and Ken. They will use the information you submit to set up computer accounts and permissions, add you to the lcp e-mail list, and order any keys that you will need.
